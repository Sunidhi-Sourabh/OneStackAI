## Vision for OneStackAI

- A curated AI launcher for productivity.
- Mobile-first.
- Solo dev project by Sunidhi Sourabh.
- Categorized tools:
    - Code
    - Art
    - Video
    - Productivity
    - Chat
- MVP: Just links to popular AI tools.
